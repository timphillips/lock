An interactive combination lock built with RxJS.

Created by Tim Phillips. Source code on GitHub.

How do single-dial combination locks work?

Reset
  • Turn the dial at least three times clockwise to the right.
Unlock
  1. Turn the dial to the right and stop at the first number.
  2. Turn the dial to the left, going past zero and the first number. Then stop at the second number.
  3. Turn the dial to the right and go directly to the last number. Click on the handle to open the lock.
